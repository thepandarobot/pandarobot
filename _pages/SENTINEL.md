# Synopsis:

<h2>Following a nuclear holocaust that sent Earth into a new Ice Age, mankind retreated into an underground metropolis called Arcadia, the upside-down vertical city where it's always night. After hundreds of years had passed, researchers started to retrieve frozen people from the surface and revive them for the sake of performing cruel experiments. Used as lab rats, the 'defrosted' are guarded by Sentinels, invulnerable chrome robots that can vaporize them in an instant. Most of the defrosted abandoned all hope, but few try to escape and journey down to level 9, where the Resistance formed in the darkest bowels of Arcadia</h2>
