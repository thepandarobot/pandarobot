Yes.

Now that we got that out of the way, let me just say this: I understand that AI generated images are a tool, and if other people can use that tool to their liking and are satisfied with the results, more power to them.

I have a certain set of standards, and I apologize in advance if I come off as arrogant. I’m not the best artist in terms of technical skill, but I like to draw, and I like to express emotion while drawing. I was an introverted kid that got picked on a lot, and the only time I was given a modicum of respect was when I got people to watch me doodle. It became my favorite way of expressing myself, me being the one that would start talking and someone else will start talking over me. When I drew, people listened. It’s not just lines, I put some thought into what goes into the finished product and the way it’s seen.


I don’t want to tell an algorithm what I want and cross my fingers, hoping it will output something close enough on the seventh try. I’d rather try (and fail) myself, thank you very much.

Just my two cents.
