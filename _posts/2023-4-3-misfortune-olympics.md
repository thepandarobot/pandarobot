Have you ever been sad? You have, haven’t you?

WELL FUCK YOU, DID YOU KNOW THAT IN AFRICA PEOPLE DIE OF AIDS AND THERE ARE PEOPLE OUT THERE IN THE WORLD WITH CANCER AND THEY DON’T EVEN HAVE A NICE IPHONE LIKE YOURS WITH GORILLA SCREEN AND FIFTY BILLION MEGAPIXEL CAMERA? WELL, NOT SO SAD NOW, HUH, SAD BOY?

If you heard stuff like that before, you have my permission (and advice) to insert the closest umbrella into that person’s anus and open it, repeatedly. Let the gas escape from that gaslighter’s ass.

The world is not the Misfortune Olympics. You have a right to be sad or angry, even if the reason is stupid. In the grand scheme of things, even Albert Einstein was a hairless monkey that wrote strange letters on a piece of paper. You can trivialize everything, especially since most things are trivial by default, so don’t let anyone tell you how to feel.

I know most of you think this is the most obvious stuff, but for people like me, more slow in the… ehm, emotional development, it takes a while to figure it out, especially on your own, doubly so after years and years of gaslighting from “friends” and “family”.

Everyone has a right to be sad. Even the Kardashians.
