I officially started doing the artwork on MILLENИIUM. The story is already mapped out in broad strokes (kinda had to do it because I need to do proper foreshadowing), but now I'm doing the character designs and soon I will start to publish on Tapas and WebToon. Here's the logline from which I began developing the story, and the character design of the main character:

*It is 1999, the last days before Y2K, and Han Sauer, a shy high school student receives a strange package containing a digital watch and a tape, claiming that the watch comes from… his future self. Turning the bezel, Han discovers that the watch has the ability to rewind time for up to a minute. He soon finds himself in a race against time to stop the Apocalypse, all while being engaged in a cat-and-mouse game with a strange woman that knows about the events that will occur at the end of the millennium.*
