Yeah, sorry to break it to you: you may be the saddest soul on earth, but human empathy dictates that even if your therapist cannot connect with you, you will go down as a sad sack of shit that nobody cared even to spit on to put out flames.

Yes, I’ve tried therapy once. My female therapist gave me a laundry list of accolades and achievements. She also turned our weekly sessions into advertisements for her 1000$ per month “group therapy sessions”, a kumbaya bullshit group that she used to extort her patients under the guise of ‘a safe environment’. And the more I tried to open up, the more she pushed on her ‘magical group’, even to the point of shaming her client for what he was confessing in private. Fuck, guess I forgot it was a job after all, and the market was harsh during that pandemic :( .

I put my soul out on a plate, exactly once. No secrets. It was during the COVID 19 pandemic, when the knife reached to the bone, and I couldn’t stand anything. Even the solitude I always craved felt suffocating. I was DONE. And I was ready for any bit of empathy, even if I had to pay for it. I was ready for artificial kindness, just to numb the pain. Even alcohol couldn’t cut it anymore, I felt like crawling up on the walls, relieving all the worst moments of my life, over and over, while in quarantine (I never even got a positive COVID test, but I had to quarantine 2 weeks because my parents got positive while I lived with them)

And then… therapy, the be-all, end-all of GEN Z healing… it fucking SUCKED! A bunch of bullshit, that’s what it was! An AD that I paid for by the hour!

It was art that never failed me. Therapy is GARBAGE, sorry but it’s true. I’ve never been failed harder.

I’m sorry to break it to y’all, but human nature is fucking flawed, and the cynical but absolute truth is the following:

You don’t even get pity unless you’re interesting.

Thank you, take care.
